# Project Implementation Roadmap: Integrity Bridge Pilot

## Quarter 1: Foundation & Local Integration (Months 1-3)
- **Technical:** Finalize the AI-logic integration for Botswana DPA and King V compliance.
- **Regulatory:** Establish a technical working group with local GRC experts to validate the "Trust Score" algorithm.
- **Outcome:** A "v0.1" automated vetting engine for Botswana-based entities.

## Quarter 2: NGO Onboarding & Vetting (Months 4-6)
- **Pilot:** Partner with 10 local NGOs in Gaborone for initial vetting.
- **AI Training:** Use the "v0.1" engine to generate real-world Integrity Scores.
- **Outcome:** A live "Trust Registry" containing 10 verified, institutional-grade organizations.

## Quarter 3: Donor Interface & "Friction" Testing (Months 7-9)
- **Feature:** Launch the Donor Dashboard allowing international philanthropists to view real-time compliance logs.
- **Integration:** Test the "Transaction Bridge" to ensure compliance data facilitates cross-border banking approvals.
- **Outcome:** Reduction in "Due Diligence Time" by a target of 70%.

## Quarter 4: SADC Scaling & Reporting (Months 10-12)
- **Scaling:** Expand the registry to 50 entities across the SADC region.
- **Grant Final Report:** Present evidence to the Gates Foundation on how AI-driven transparency increased donation volume.
- **Outcome:** A sustainable, scalable RegTech platform for the African philanthropic market.
