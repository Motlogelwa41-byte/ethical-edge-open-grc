## 4. Privacy by Design
- **Data Residency:** All sensitive data will be stored on servers compliant with Botswana’s Data Protection Act, ensuring sovereign control over NGO records.
- **Minimum Data Collection:** The AI only analyzes governance markers (e.g., presence of an audit committee) rather than personal donor identities.

## 5. Algorithmic Accountability
- **Explainable AI (XAI):** The engine provides a "Reasoning Code" for every Trust Score. If an NGO receives a 'Low' score, the system generates a roadmap of specific King V principles they need to improve.
- **Fairness Audit:** Quarterly reviews will be conducted to ensure the AI does not develop a bias toward larger, city-based organizations over rural community groups.
