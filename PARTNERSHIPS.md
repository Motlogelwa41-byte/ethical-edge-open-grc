# Strategic Partnerships & Ecosystem Alignment

## 1. Pilot Partner Strategy
Ethical Edge GRC is currently engaging with local Gaborone-based NGOs in the **Education and Youth Development** sectors. We are selecting partners who meet the following criteria:
- Minimum of 3 years of operational history in Botswana.
- Commitment to adopting digital transparency standards.
- Alignment with the "Nal Se Jal" or similar infrastructure/social impact missions.

## 2. Institutional Collaborators
- **Governmental:** Aligning with the **Public Procurement Regulatory Authority (PPRA)** to ensure our 'Trust Score' reflects national integrity standards.
- **Technical:** Potential collaboration with the **University of Botswana (Computer Science Dept)** for independent verification of our AI vetting algorithms.

## 3. Data Partners
- We intend to utilize data from **CIPA** and the **Botswana Bureau of Standards (BOBS)** to cross-reference the legal and quality status of vetted entities.
